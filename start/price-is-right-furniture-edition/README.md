# Price Is Right (Furniture Edition)

Pricing challenge where players guess if an item is above/below a price or pick the closest value.

## Gameplay
- Show a featured item with price options (above/below or multiple choice).
- Encourage quick decisions with large, kiosk-friendly buttons.
- Offer streak bonuses for consecutive correct answers.

## Controls
- Huge tap targets for primary choices; minimal text for fast reads.
- Optional slider mode for advanced rounds.

## Scoring
- Points for correct guesses; streak multiplier for consecutive wins.
- Time bonus for fast responses to keep the pace lively.

## Content and branding
- Use FD MSRP or price range buckets to keep content flexible.
- Rotate featured items by department (living, bedroom, outdoor).
