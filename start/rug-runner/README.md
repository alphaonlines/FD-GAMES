# Rug Runner

Endless runner where players roll out a rug, avoid obstacles, and collect décor pickups.

## Gameplay
- Control a rolling rug as it moves forward.
- Avoid obstacles while collecting pillows, throws, or other décor.
- Introduce combo pickups to reward precise paths.

## Controls
- Swipe to shift lanes or steer; optional tilt alternative if supported.
- Simple jump/slide buttons if obstacles vary by height.

## Scoring
- Distance traveled plus combo pickup bonuses.
- Streak bonuses for consecutive pickups without hits.

## Content and branding
- Use rug and décor art from FD collections.
- Seasonal obstacle sets (holiday decor, outdoor hazards) keep it fresh.
